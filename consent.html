Research Participation

Rothamsted Satellite Weed Mapping project

You've been invited to take part in a research project. Here's what's involved.

What we'd share

Black-grass distribution maps from 1–4 of your fields that Rothamsted has already mapped. This data goes to a satellite imaging company (https://geco-ag.com/) who will use it to build better weed detection models.

What you get back

You will receive satellite-derived weed maps showing where weed patches are across your fields, plus guidance on where you could reduce herbicide applications and save on spray costs.

Your privacy

Only weed mapping data is shared — no personal details, no contact information, no farm business data. Your participation is identified only by an anonymous code.

Your rights

You can withdraw at any time by contacting the research team here. Your data will be removed on request.
